Mt. Apo Trek

Mt. Apo is the highest mountain in the Philippines, rising to an elevation of 2,954 meters (9,692 feet) above sea level. It boasts breathtaking views, rich biodiversity, and a challenging ascent, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Sleeping Dinosaur

Sleeping Dinosaur is a popular tourist spot in Mati City, Philippines, known for its natural rock formation that resembles a sleeping dinosaur when viewed from a specific angle. The site offers a unique and picturesque landscape, attracting visitors seeking memorable photos.
